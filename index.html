About us

Flutterby Tea, the tea shop that brings nature to you!

Come and enjoy a cup of our ethically sourced, diverse teas in our green courtyard, surrounded by live species of butterflies. We're not just a tea shop - we're an urban oasis offering a forest-themed escape in the heart of the city. Experience the beauty of nature while sipping responsibly and supporting butterfly conservation.

Live butterfly species

Step into our enchanting organic tea shop and immerse yourself in the mesmerizing world of live butterfly species. Witness the vibrant fluttering wings of nature's delicate artistry as these graceful creatures dance around our carefully curated butterfly garden. Discover a diverse array of species, each one a testament to the awe-inspiring beauty and intricate wonders of the natural world. Our live butterfly species offer a truly captivating experience that will leave you feeling connected to the extraordinary wonders of nature.

Ethical tea sourcing

At Flutterby, we are passionate about providing you with the finest organic teas while ensuring ethical practices from farm to cup. We meticulously source our teas from sustainable, certified organic gardens around the world. By prioritizing fair trade principles and supporting local communities, we create a positive impact on the lives of tea farmers and workers. With every sip, you can enjoy our hand-selected teas, knowing that they are grown with utmost care and respect for both people and the environment.

Sustainability

At Flutterby, we are committed to promoting sustainability in every aspect of our business. From the moment our tea leaves are sourced until they reach your cup, we strive to minimize our ecological footprint. We work closely with our suppliers to ensure environmentally friendly cultivation practices, such as organic farming methods and natural pest control. Our packaging materials are carefully chosen to be eco-friendly and recyclable. By embracing sustainable practices, we aim to protect our planet and preserve the natural beauty that inspires us every day.
